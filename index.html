The Prayers of the Rosary

Before beginning the Mysteries, recite these prayers in order.

1. The Sign of the Cross In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.
2. The Apostles' Creed I believe in God, the Father Almighty, Creator of heaven and earth, and in Jesus Christ, His only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died and was buried; He descended into hell; on the third day He rose again from the dead; He ascended into heaven, and is seated at the right hand of God the Father Almighty; from there He will come to judge the living and the dead. I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy Catholic Church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and life everlasting. Amen.
3. The Our Father Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be Thy name; Thy kingdom come; Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread; and forgive us our trespasses as we forgive those who trespass against us; and lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. Amen.
4. The Hail Mary Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee; blessed art thou among women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners, now and at the hour of our death. Amen.
5. The Glory Be Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit. As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.
6. The Fatima Prayer (O My Jesus) O my Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of hell, and lead all souls to Heaven, especially those in most need of Thy mercy.

Meditate on the Mysteries

Select the set of mysteries for today to see the meditations.

The Joyful Mysteries

Theme: The Incarnation. Focus on the humility of Mary and the childhood of Jesus.

1. The Annunciation
Focus on: Humility. The Angel Gabriel appears to Mary, and she says "Yes" to God's will.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

2. The Visitation
Focus on: Charity/Love of Neighbor. Mary visits her cousin Elizabeth, who is pregnant with John the Baptist.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

3. The Nativity
Focus on: Poverty of Spirit. Jesus is born in a stable in Bethlehem. God becomes man.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

4. The Presentation
Focus on: Obedience. Mary and Joseph present the baby Jesus in the Temple.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

5. The Finding in the Temple
Focus on: Piety/Joy in finding Jesus. After being lost for three days, Jesus is found teaching in the temple.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

The Luminous Mysteries

Theme: Christ's Public Ministry. Focus on Jesus bringing light to the world.

1. The Baptism in the Jordan
Focus on: Openness to the Holy Spirit. The heavens open and the Father proclaims, "This is my beloved Son."

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

2. The Wedding at Cana
Focus on: To Jesus through Mary. At Mary's request, Jesus performs His first miracle, turning water into wine.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

3. The Proclamation of the Kingdom
Focus on: Repentance and Trust in God. Jesus preaches the Gospel and calls all to conversion.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

4. The Transfiguration
Focus on: Desire for Holiness. Jesus is transfigured on Mount Tabor, revealing His divine glory to the Apostles.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

5. The Institution of the Eucharist
Focus on: Adoration. At the Last Supper, Jesus gives us His Body and Blood in the Holy Eucharist.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

The Sorrowful Mysteries

Theme: The Passion. Focus on the suffering Jesus endured for our sins.

1. The Agony in the Garden
Focus on: True Contrition for Sin. Jesus sweats blood praying to the Father in Gethsemane.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

2. The Scourging at the Pillar
Focus on: Purity/Mortification. Jesus is lashed with whips, enduring terrible physical pain.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

3. The Crowning with Thorns
Focus on: Moral Courage. A crown of thorns is mocked and pressed into His head.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

4. The Carrying of the Cross
Focus on: Patience. Jesus carries the heavy wood to Calvary, falling three times.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

5. The Crucifixion
Focus on: Salvation/Forgiveness. Jesus dies on the cross to save the world.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

The Glorious Mysteries

Theme: The Resurrection. Focus on the triumph of God and eternal life.

1. The Resurrection
Focus on: Faith. Jesus rises from the dead, conquering sin and death forever.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

2. The Ascension
Focus on: Hope. Jesus ascends into Heaven to take His place at the right hand of the Father.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

3. The Descent of the Holy Spirit
Focus on: Love of God/Wisdom. The Holy Spirit comes down upon Mary and the Apostles at Pentecost.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

4. The Assumption
Focus on: Grace of a Happy Death. The Virgin Mary is taken up body and soul into Heaven.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

5. The Coronation of Mary
Focus on: Trust in Mary's Intercession. Mary is crowned Queen of Heaven and Earth.

Pray: 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, 1 Glory Be, 1 Fatima Prayer.

Concluding Prayer: Hail Holy Queen (Salve Regina) Hail, holy Queen, Mother of mercy, hail, our life, our sweetness and our hope. To thee do we cry, poor banished children of Eve: to thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this valley of tears. Turn then, most gracious Advocate, thine eyes of mercy toward us, and after this our exile, show unto us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus, O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary.

V. Pray for us, O holy Mother of God.
R. That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.

O Mary, conceived without sin, pray for us who have recourse to thee.
